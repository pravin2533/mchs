About Us :

The dream of pursuing higher education in prestigious universities across the globe is often impeded by two daunting challenges: standardized tests like the GRE, SAT, TOEFL, and IELTS, and the complex and lengthy admission process for international students. To address these challenges, I established Dilip Oak’s Academy in 1996 with the mission to assist deserving students in overcoming these hurdles and realizing their academic aspirations.
Our ultimate goal at Dilip Oak’s Academy is to serve as the gateway to higher education overseas, providing a solid foundation for future success for students from all backgrounds, including those from middle-class and lower-middle-class families. We strive to ensure that no deserving student is denied the opportunity to pursue their dream of studying abroad due to these obstacles.
Through our comprehensive training and support services, we aim to equip students with the skills and knowledge needed to excel in their standardized tests, navigate the admission process, and ultimately secure admission to their dream university. Our team of experienced and dedicated educators is committed to providing personalized guidance and support to each student, ensuring that they are well-prepared and confident to tackle these challenges.
At Dilip Oak’s Academy, we believe that education is the key to unlocking a bright and prosperous future, and we are dedicated to helping our students achieve their full potential.

Whilst studying at a foreign university is an excellent option, it intrigues the prospective student with many a variety of questions. Your doubts start right from scratch as “What course should I do?”; Which country should I study in?”; “Should I go to a University or a College?” to some of the finer points such as, “Will I get a Good Job after completing the course?”; “Will it fit my budget?” etc.
These queries cannot be answered better by anyone than those who have “been there and done that”. Yes, our handpicked teams of counselors themselves have gone through the grind of studying abroad and have first-hand experiences to share with you, the prospective applicants. They know not only what courses you have the options to choose from, but also the best food around campus, the culture, and any and every detail you would need of your future alma mater.
